Anchor

A quiet companion for staying with one intention.

Start a session, choose how you want Anchor to check in, and keep focus visible without turning the work into another dashboard.

See the flow Privacy
01

Pick your proctor.

Choose the style of check-in you want. Some proctors can use an optional goal, while others can start immediately.

02

Start when ready.

Set the timer and begin. If the selected proctor needs a work target, add it before starting.

03

Stay in the session.

The main view stays small: timer, goal, and current state. Anchor checks context in the background and only speaks up when it matters.

04

Recover quickly.

When Anchor sees clear drift, it gives a short reminder instead of a long report.

05

Finish with a trace.

Session results and history stay readable: enough context to learn from the block, not enough clutter to live there.

Companion

Simple enough to leave open.

Anchor is designed to feel like a small native companion: visible when useful, quiet the rest of the time.

Privacy

Made for your Mac, not for surveillance.

No analytics, ads, tracking, or telemetry services.

Local settings, history, and model files stay on your device unless you choose to share them.

Optional third-party AI services are used only when you configure and use those features.
