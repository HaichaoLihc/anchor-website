Local-first focus for macOS

Anchor

Choose a proctor. Stay on task. Earn a pet.

Anchor pairs each focus block with a proctor that checks your session, sends a short nudge when you drift, and rewards strong scores with collectible pets.

01

Choose the proctor.

Pick the check-in style for the session. The proctor watches for drift while you stay with the work.

02

Stay with the work.

The session view stays simple: timer, score, proctor state, and a short note about how the block is going.

03

Get nudged back.

When Anchor sees clear drift, it sends a short reminder with the next concrete action.

04

See what happened.

Results summarize the score and the outcome. A high enough score can unlock a new pet; a weaker block simply shows where the session drifted.

05

Keep a readable history.

Session history keeps the score, selected proctor, drift line, and event trace together so the next block starts with useful context.

Pets

Build a collection from better focus sessions.

Pets are rewards, not monitors. Score high enough in a session and Anchor can add a new companion to your collection.

Privacy

Made for your Mac, not for surveillance.

No analytics, ads, tracking, or telemetry services.

Local settings, history, and model files stay on your device unless you choose to share them.

Optional third-party AI services are used only when you configure and use those features.
