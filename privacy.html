Anchor

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Anchor is a macOS focus companion designed to run locally on your Mac. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data.

Data We Collect

Anchor does not collect personal data on our servers. We do not operate analytics, advertising, tracking, or telemetry services for Anchor.

Local App Data

Anchor may store focus session settings, session history, app preferences, downloaded local model files, and related app state on your Mac. This data stays on your device unless you choose to share it outside the app.

Screen Recording, Camera, and Notifications

Anchor may request macOS permissions such as Screen Recording, Camera, and Notifications. These permissions are used to provide focus-session features on your device. Screen contents and camera input are processed locally for local proctor modes.

Optional Third-Party Services

Anchor may include optional features that use third-party AI services if you configure and use them. When you use a third-party service, information needed for that feature may be sent to that provider and handled under that provider's privacy policy. You can avoid this by using Anchor's local modes.

Data Retention and Deletion

Anchor's local data remains on your Mac until you delete it. You can delete local Anchor data by removing the app and its related files from your Mac's Application Support and cache folders.

Children's Privacy

Anchor is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
